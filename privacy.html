Privacy Policy

Effective: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Munnin (the "Service") handles information. Munnin is a personal reminder tool operated by Corey Artim ("Operator") in Dayton, OH. The Service is operated for personal use; SMS messages are sent only to the Operator's own verified phone number.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Munnin runs in the Operator's web browser. It does not operate a server, database, or user accounts. As a result:

  • No personal information is collected, transmitted, or stored on any server controlled by the Operator.
  • No analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, cookies for tracking, or third-party trackers are used.
  • No information is sold, shared, rented, or otherwise disclosed to any third party for marketing purposes.

2. Information Stored Locally

Reminder data (task titles, due dates, categories, completion history) is stored exclusively in the Operator's web browser using the browser's built-in storage. This data never leaves the Operator's device except when explicitly transmitted to the third-party services described below.

3. Third-Party Services Used

The Service uses the following third-party services to function. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Twilio — used to send SMS reminders to the Operator's phone number. Reminder content (task title, due date, optional notes) is transmitted to Twilio for delivery. See Twilio's privacy policy.
  • Anthropic — used optionally to process natural-language commands entered by the Operator into the in-app chat. Chat messages are transmitted to Anthropic for processing. See Anthropic's privacy policy.
  • JSONBin.io — used optionally to receive SMS reply commands. Incoming SMS reply text and sender phone number are stored briefly in a private JSONBin "bin" controlled by the Operator. See JSONBin's privacy policy.

API keys for these services are stored in the Operator's browser storage and used solely to access these services on the Operator's behalf.

4. SMS Messages

SMS messages are sent only to the verified phone number configured by the Operator. The Service does not send SMS messages to any other recipient. Message frequency varies based on the Operator's configured reminders. Standard message and data rates from the recipient's wireless carrier may apply.

To opt out, the Operator may at any time disable SMS in the Service settings, reply STOP to any message, or deactivate the Twilio number used by the Service.

5. Data Retention

Reminder data persists in the Operator's browser storage until the Operator clears it. Chat history is capped to the most recent 50 turns. Inbound SMS replies stored at JSONBin are typically cleared by the Service within seconds of processing and capped at 50 messages.

6. Security

All communication with third-party services occurs over HTTPS. The Operator is responsible for protecting the device on which the Service runs, including device-level access controls.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is operated for personal use by an adult and is not intended for use by children under 13. No data is knowingly collected from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective" date above.
